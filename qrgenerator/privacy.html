Privacy Policy — QR Generator

Effective Date: June 21, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how Marto Studio collects, uses, and protects information in the QR Generator mobile application.

1. Information We Collect

Our application does not request, collect, or store any personally identifiable information (such as names, email addresses, or phone numbers) from its users.

2. Third-Party Services

To improve the user experience and analyze performance, we use the following services:

  • Google Play Services: For the core functionality of the application.
  • Google Analytics for Firebase: To obtain anonymous statistics about the general performance of the application.
  • Firebase Crashlytics: For anonymous real-time crash detection and reporting.
  • Mixpanel: For analyzing usage events and behavior (button clicks, types of QR created, etc.) solely to improve the interface and features. No personal or identifiable data is collected.

3. Permissions and Internet Access

The application requires the following permissions to function properly:

  • Camera: Required only to capture and scan QR codes in real-time.
  • Storage / Gallery: Required only to allow reading QR codes from images saved on the device.
  • Internet Access: Required only to interact with the third-party services mentioned in the previous section.

4. Contact Us

If you have any questions, you can contact us through the app store or by writing to: contact@martostudio.com
